---
title: "Paper Reviews"
description: "Analytical reviews of research papers in geometric vision, 3D scene representation, teleoperation, manipulation, and humanoid systems."
---

Reviews are written to extract what is technically significant and to connect methods to open implementation questions. The emphasis is on geometric and algorithmic structure, not summary.

Topics covered: 3D Gaussian Splatting, object-centric representations, teleoperation, demonstration reuse, part-level 3D understanding, humanoid loco-manipulation, and digital twin systems.
